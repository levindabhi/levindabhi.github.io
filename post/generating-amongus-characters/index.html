Generating Amongus Characters

Results of training stylegan2-ADA with few images

Levin Dabhi
Feb 7, 2021 2 min read

After one of my tweet went semi-viral, I motivated myself to put together the results of my stylegan2-ADA experiment in this post. Adding augmentation in discriminator of stylegan2 was cherry on the cake if one wants to train stylegan2 for the limited number of images.

In StyleganADA paper authors explored small datasets with around 2000 to 1500 images. I decided to find the lower limit and trained on a few hundred images.

I trained on three categories namely teddy bear faces, amongus characters, lego images. I used the original stylegan2-ADA repo TensorFlow version for training. Main points that make the training successful without mode collapse are

  1. Right Initial point Fine-tuning from weight file which is close to your data helps a lot
  2. Augmentation Strength If your dataset contains a few hundred images it better to start with high augment strength and keep target low to ramp up augment strength speedily.
  3. Learning rate and Gamma Its better to explore few values Learning rate and Gamma before starting full training. Also, try different values when mode collapse occurs.

Below are details of a dataset, weight file link and results of each category. Hope you have a fun time watching results!
